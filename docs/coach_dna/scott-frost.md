# Scott Frost (UCF HC / play-caller) — Public Qualitative Record

*BYU 2026 opponent: week 8. Play-caller identity verified in
`docs/play_caller_checklist.md` (Tier 2) — Frost calls, OC Cooper
coordinates. Compiled 2026-07-15 from web research; every claim carries its
source and grade.*

## Philosophy & identity
- CONFIRMED — Frost reinstated the "UCFast" branding on his 2025 return: "UCFast (signage) is still up all over the building. I really appreciate everybody for leaving some of those things up because I didn't have to reinvent a lot." (On3/UCFSports, Brandon Helwig, 2025-07-28: https://www.on3.com/sites/ucf-sports/news/ucfast-offense-returns-ucf-2025-camp/)
- CONFIRMED — "I think we've got the type of athletes here that fit me and fit our offense well. If we can max those guys out, then we'll look like a fast offense." (same On3 article, 2025-07-28)
- SECONDHAND — Beat coverage frames the 2025-26 offense as the same tempo-spread identity from his 2016-17 stint, with flexibility depending on personnel (SI, Bryson Turner, 2025-07-30: https://www.si.com/college/ucf/ucf-s-scott-frost-gives-extensive-break-down-of-offensive-philosophy-01k1d165mew8)

## Tempo
- CONFIRMED — "Fast can mean a lot of different things... but first I want to execute at a fast pace and within plays." And: "Less than full speed doesn't get it done. There's too many talented guys out there." (On3, 2025-07-28, URL above; near-identical wording in SI, 2025-07-30)
- CONFIRMED — "I would rather have a team that goes 100 miles an hour and 100 percent all the time and let us pull the reins back than a team that gets used to doing things less than full speed." (On3, preseason camp, July 2025: https://www.on3.com/sites/ucf-sports/news/scott-frost-ucf-preseason-camp-2025/)
- CONFIRMED — Tempo lapses drew criticism from Frost himself in Aug 2025: team was "a little lax" between plays; "We've talked to the guys about urgency and getting set, getting lined up... and didn't see enough of it tonight." (SI, first fall scrimmage: https://www.si.com/college/ucf/scott-frost-s-three-takeaways-from-ucf-s-first-fall-scrimmage-01k2401c9yjn)

## Risk & aggression
- CONFIRMED — Frost tied aggression to complementary football: "If our defense is as good as I think it could be, we might be a little more careful on offense." But "If we need to go score a bunch of points to win, then we'll do our best to do that." (On3, 2025-07-28; SI, 2025-07-30)
- No public statements found on fourth-down or two-point philosophy specifically.

## Play-calling process
- CONFIRMED (caller-supplied context; original URL not independently located by the research agent) — 2025 reporting stated Frost would be primary play-caller with OC Steve Cooper contributing to game planning. 247Sports' Cooper profile says Cooper will "direct the offense" but does not assign play-calling (https://247sports.com/college/central-florida/article/who-is-ucf-offensive-coordinator-steve-cooper-251401882/).
- SECONDHAND — Media debated delegation mid-2025 season: Locked On UCF episode "Scott Frost's Play-Calling DILEMMA | Is UCF's Head Coach Stretching Himself Too Thin?" (2025-10-25: https://www.wtsp.com/video/sports/locked-on/lo-orlando/locked-on-ucf/scott-frosts-play-calling-dilemma-is-ucfs-head-coach-stretching-himself-too-thin-i-ucf-podcast/535-006229f2-df01-486f-b2e1-53262e7af8db). No handoff announced. **Attribution watch item:** if 2026 struggles revive the delegation debate, re-verify the caller in-season.
- SECONDHAND — For 2026, Cooper Bassett was hired as TE coach so Cooper "can focus fully on coordinating and developing the offense" (paraphrase) — structural, not a play-calling change (https://roundtable.io/sports/ncaa/ucf/news/continuity-is-an-underrated-strength-of-2026-ucf-knights).

## Influences & tree
- CONFIRMED (dated) — "My time at Oregon with Chip, and then taking his offense and continuing to evolve it, that's really what's gotten us to where we are." (2017-11-29, USA Football: https://blogs.usafootball.com/blog/4999/how-scott-frost-evolved-chip-kelly-s-offense-at-central-florida)
- CONFIRMED — On his 2024 Rams analyst year: "It brought me back... you forget how fun it is to be around the game." "I'm so grateful to those guys." (ESPN, Andrea Adelson, 2025-05-05: https://www.espn.com/college-football/story/_/id/44980213/college-football-scott-frost-return-ucf)
- No recent (2025-26) public statements found naming Kelly directly.

## Change since Nebraska & 2026 outlook
- CONFIRMED — "I know I'm a wiser person and smarter football coach. When you're young, you think you have it all figured out." Also refuses Nebraska specifics: "I'm never going to talk about that." (ESPN, 2025-05-05)
- CONFIRMED — "Don't take the wrong job." (via Chris Vannini/The Athletic, quoted in SI, 2025-07-17: https://www.si.com/college/ucf/ucf-s-scott-frost-holds-nothing-back-when-discussing-issues-at-nebraska-01k0chtyrjkz)
- CONFIRMED — 2026 spring: "We're really going to focus on fundamentals and details this spring, not open up playbooks on either side too much, and just make sure we're good at the simple things." (247Sports spring camp transcript: https://247sports.com/college/central-florida/article/everything-scott-frost-said-to-open-ucfs-2026-spring-camp-279780460/)
- CONFIRMED — Big 12 Media Days 2026: "I'm certainly a lot more confident in this team than I was a year ago"; on QB Alonza Barnett III: "It's gonna be nice and refreshing to have a guy that everybody looks to and knows that he's the guy." (On3, 2026-07-07: https://www.on3.com/sites/ucf-sports/news/scott-frost-ucf-big-12-media-days-2026/)
