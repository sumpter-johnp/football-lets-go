## Philosophy & identity
- CONFIRMED — "I've always wanted a system that has that type of versatility to cater it to the personnel." (Deseret News Q&A, Feb 19, 2026)
- CONFIRMED — "I want us to be efficient. I want us to be innovative, and I want us to stress the defense" ... "It's all about getting the best usage out of our personnel" (KSL.com, Mar 27, 2026)
- CONFIRMED — "We're going to lean heavily on the run game...that's Utah football and Morgan Scalley doesn't want it any other way." (Deseret, Feb 19, 2026)
- CONFIRMED — Continuity, not teardown: "You are not coming in necessarily trying to rebuild something that was broken because it wasn't broken." (Deseret, Feb 19, 2026); "I would be remiss if I wasn't studying last year's offense" (KSL.com, Mar 27, 2026)
- CONFIRMED — Culture directive from Scalley: match the defense's identity — "that same type of toughness, that same type of relentlessness on the offensive side" (247Sports, Mar 25, 2026); Scalley: "I want to dominate the line of scrimmage, I want to be innovative, and I want to stress defensive coordinators." (Deseret, Mar 26, 2026)
- SECONDHAND — Beat writers characterize it as a Beck-like flexible, RPO-heavy, QB-run-centric system with creative formations; Scalley on the hire: "His ability to innovate and adapt to different personnel is extremely impressive..." (KSL Sports, Jan 3, 2026)

## Tempo
- SECONDHAND only — KSL Sports hire announcement (Jan 3, 2026, Steve Bartle) says his units win with "tempo and efficiency," "often pushing tempo." No direct McGiven quotes on tempo found.

## Risk & aggression
- CONFIRMED (thin) — On QB arm talent limits: "I would never really shy away from any calls with either of those guys." (247Sports spring-close piece, ~Apr 2026)
- CONFIRMED — QB athleticism as a play-caller's escape hatch: "They can get you out of certain situations. It's always nice as a play-caller." (Deseret, Mar 26, 2026; also 247Sports, Mar 25, 2026)
- No public statements found on fourth-down/shot-play appetite specifically.

## Play-calling process
- CONFIRMED — Attack via layers: "...just different layers any way you can to attack a defense and trying to dictate a game that way." (Deseret, Feb 19, 2026)
- CONFIRMED — QB-development-driven: "Developing the total quarterback...developing their decision making, developing their processes"; moving QBs "from being told, 'throw it here,' to working through full-field progressions" (Deseret Mar 26 / KSL Mar 25, 2026)
- CONFIRMED — Personnel packages: intrigued by Utah's hybrid "rhino" tight ends — "I haven't really played with the rhino...how do you utilize those guys?" (KSL.com Mar 27 / Deseret Mar 26, 2026)

## 2026 outlook
- CONFIRMED — Devon Dampier IS at Utah under McGiven (did not follow Beck out): extensive spring quotes — "The command that he's developed over the last couple of practices has been really impressive to see..." (247Sports spring close); "the possibilities with them are endless" (Deseret, Feb 19). NFL framing: "How do we need to develop you to get you to the next level? We need to get you more in tune with protection." (247Sports, Mar 25, 2026)
