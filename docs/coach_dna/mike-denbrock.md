# Mike Denbrock — Public Record (Notre Dame OC, 2024–26)

*BYU 2026 opponent: week 7. Play-caller identity verified in
`docs/play_caller_checklist.md` (Tier 2). Compiled 2026-07-15 from web
research; every claim carries its source and grade.*

## Philosophy & identity
- "I think everything that you do has to be built around a strong running game," and on run calls: "when we do call runs that we give our guys a chance, numbers-wise, number one." — CONFIRMED (SI, Feb 26, 2024: https://www.si.com/college/notredame/football/notre-dame-notebook-mike-denbrock-talks-national-championships-running-game-head-coach-desire-stires)
- But not dogmatic: "I'm more open than I was years ago to not just pound my head against a brick wall," and "the more athletes you can get out in space and create mismatches is also a good way to play." — CONFIRMED (same SI article, Feb 26, 2024)
- Personnel-first identity: "There's things that just fit your personnel better. And that's as much my job as anything else, is to make sure what we end up looking like offensively fits who are the guys that we're asking to do those jobs." — CONFIRMED (Blue & Gold/On3 summer 2025 sit-down: https://www.on3.com/teams/notre-dame-fighting-irish/news/everything-notre-dame-oc-mike-denbrock-said-in-summer-sit-down-with-blue-gold/)

## Tempo
- "We want to play with a tempo and a pace to what we do, that keeps the pressure on the defense and play from a position of strength." — CONFIRMED (On3, Aug 1, 2024 fall-camp quick hitters: https://www.on3.com/teams/notre-dame-fighting-irish/news/notre-dame-football-offensive-coordinator-mike-denbrock-quick-hitters-fighting-irish-quotes-quarterback-riley-leonard/). Search snippets attribute a further quote about mixing tempo (signal/huddle/fast-to-line) to him, but the agent could not verify it against a specific page — treat as unverified.

## Risk & aggression
- On QB aggressiveness (re: CJ Carr, before 2025 Miami opener): "It's I think easier, in my opinion, to say 'whoa' than it is 'giddy up' with a quarterback. And I don't have that problem with him for sure." — CONFIRMED (247Sports pre-Miami transcript, ~Aug/Sept 2025: https://247sports.com/college/notre-dame/longformarticle/everything-notre-dame-fighting-irish-offensive-coordinator-mike-denbrock-said-on-wednesday-before-no-10-miami-253061417/)
- 4th-down aggression is driven by HC Freeman: "If I tell our group that we want to be aggressive and attacking, I have to be that way as a coach. You got to live with sometimes, on 4th-and-2, you didn't get it." — CONFIRMED, Freeman quote (On3 postgame, Texas A&M, Sept 2024: https://www.on3.com/teams/notre-dame-fighting-irish/news/what-notre-dame-fighting-irish-football-marcus-freeman-said-after-texas-am-game-2024/; analysis: https://www.ndinsider.com/story/sports/football/2024/09/29/analysis-notre-dame-football-masters-fourth-down-on-both-sides-marcus-freeman-riley-leonard/75442344007/)
- Deep shots are built into normal progression reads rather than isolated calls; 2025 offense led FBS at 7.32 yards/play (paraphrase). — SECONDHAND (Board Drill: https://www.boarddrill.com/p/explosive-by-design-how-notre-dame)

## Play-calling process
- Denbrock calls games from the sideline (2024–), with QB coach Gino Guidugli in the booth as his eyes (paraphrase). — SECONDHAND (On3, Aug 29, 2024: https://www.on3.com/news/notre-dame-coordinators-mike-denbrock-al-golden-on-sideline-2024-season/)
- QB check authority — CJ Carr: "They give us a lot of freedom to check plays"; "We're very active at the line of scrimmage"; "All the protections are on us." — CONFIRMED via search index of Irish Sports Daily, June/July 2026 (page 403s on fetch): https://irishsportsdaily.com/s/27825/cj-carr-reflects-on-notre-dames-resilience-expanded-role-at-manning-passing-academy
- No public statements found on scripting.

## Influences & tree
- Brian Kelly: GA roommates at Grand Valley State (1987); OC under Kelly there 1992–95; later Kelly's ND staff. Hired by Luke Fickell as Cincinnati OC (2017) — SECONDHAND (Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Denbrock; ESPN: https://www.espn.com/college-football/story/_/id/39167984/sources-lsu-mike-denbrock-returning-notre-dame-oc)
- On independence from Kelly's system: "I was able to break away and kind of develop my own way of doing things"; "I think just experience is the best teacher." — CONFIRMED (SI, Feb 26, 2024, above)

## Evolution & 2026 outlook
- 2025 (freshman Carr): phased install — screens/quick game early, shot plays added (Purdue), full progression reads by midseason ("training wheels" off vs. Arkansas: 354 yds, 4 TD); Carr finished 24:6 TD-INT. — SECONDHAND (Board Drill, May 4, 2026: https://www.boarddrill.com/p/notre-dame-qb-evolution-graduating)
- 2026: Freeman on returning Carr/Denbrock/Guidugli continuity: "He's able to see what the defense is doing and put us in a position that usually ends up in a positive play... That's invaluable to have a true problem solver." — CONFIRMED (UHND, Apr 23, 2026: https://www.uhnd.com/football/2026/04/23/cj-carrs-growth-signals-new-era-for-notre-dame-offense/)
- No public statements found from Denbrock himself detailing specific 2026 schematic changes.
